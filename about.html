Who Are We?

Our Company

Lumiere is a Junior Achievement Company composed of twenty five students from the Calgary and Airdrie areas. We aim to provide our customers with quality, L.E.D holiday cards that not only carry a wonderful message but also brighten someone’s day. Together, not just as a company but as a team we believe that we have the work ethic and motivation to create a company that is tremendously successful. We work well together, and each member brings a different skillset to the table. The management team is made up of nine individuals who all have different experiences with leadership. The amount of diverse skillsets in our company will ensure that our company is innovative, efficient and successful.

Lumiere

Junior Achievement

Junior Achievement is an organization that is dedicated to teaching youth the principles of financial literacy, entrepreneurial skills, and many other aspects of the business world. Our company is a branch of Junior Achievement Company Program, an eighteen week program that allows students to plan, create, and sell their own products. As a part of this, we are guided by mentors at the University of Calgary location that guide our education and help our company succeed!

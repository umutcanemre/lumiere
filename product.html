Glocards

What Are They?

Glocards are greeting cards incorporated with LED lights. Our product, L.E.D cards, are handcrafted greeting cards that are made with unique and innovative materials. Our line of LED cards are made of ivory card stock paper, LED lights, copper tape, and batteries. The LED cards currently come in christmas themed designs on the ivory colored cardstock. We plan on introducing other variations of the themes (such as birthday and Valentines Day cards) later on in the year.
